Geocaching Belt Qualifications
 WHITE: This is awarded if a cacher doesn't qualify for another colour belt
 YELLOW: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 100 cache finds (any type) AND
- 5 cache hides
 ORANGE: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 300 cache finds (any type) AND
- 10 cache hides
 GREEN: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 500 cache finds (any type) AND
- 15 cache hides AND
- 1 event (any type) attended AND
- at least one cache find in 2 distinct states
 BLUE: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 1000 cache finds (any type) AND
- 20 cache hides AND
- 3 events (any type) attended
- at least one cache find in 4 distinct states
 BROWN: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 2000 cache finds (any type) AND
- 50 cache hides AND
- 3 events (any type) attended AND
- at least one cache find in 8 distinct states AND
- hosted one event (any type) AND
- any 5 of the following conditions are met:
-- at least 50 FTFs
-- at least one find in 4 distinct countries
-- at least 72 out of the 81 D/T combinations completed (89%)
-- one 5/5 D/T rated cache
-- 30 consecutive days with a cache find
-- 50 caches found on a single day
-- Maximum distance in a day of 500km or more
-- 3 gold badges for container size
-- at least one cache found above 1000m elevation
-- 3 distinct types of caches hidden (any types incl. events)
 BLACK: This awarded if the following conditions are met:
- 3000 cache finds (any type) AND
- 50 cache hides AND
- 3 events (any type) attended AND
- at least one cache find in 8 distinct states AND
- hosted one event (any type) AND
- any 8 of the following conditions are met:
-- at least 50 FTFs
-- at least one find in 8 distinct countries
-- 81 D/T combinations completed (100%)
-- one 5/5 D/T rated cache
-- 90 consecutive days with a cache find
-- 50 caches found on a single day
-- Maximum distance in a day of 500km or more
-- 3 gold badges for container size
-- at least one cache found above 1000m elevation
-- 5 distinct types of caches hidden (any types incl. events)
